# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-04-12*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** Elevating outdoor living with premium, DIY-friendly bioclimatic aluminum pergola kits.
**What it does:** Brealu designs and manufactures high-end aluminum pergola kits, carports, and shading systems that transform outdoor spaces into functional, weather-resistant luxury environments. Our modular "Professional DIY" technology allows homeowners and contractors in Europe and North America to install a bespoke bioclimatic system with ease.
**Product category:** Outdoor Living & Shading Systems
**Product type:** E-commerce / Manufacturing (B2B & DTC)
**Business model:** Direct-to-Consumer (DTC) for premium homeowners and specialized B2B for architects, contractors, and hospitality.

## Target Audience
**Target Markets:** Europe (France, Germany, Denmark, Slovenia) and North America (USA, Canada).
**Decision-makers:** Homeowners (DTC), Architects, Interior Designers, General Contractors, Purchasing Managers.
**Primary use case:** Extending usable living space with a high-end, easy-to-install DIY pergola kit that meets local engineering standards.
**Jobs to be done:**
- "Hire" a DIY pergola kit to transform a patio into an all-season room.
- "Hire" a modular shading system to add value to a residential property.
- "Hire" a professional-grade kit to avoid the high cost of local custom builds.

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** High-end outdoor transformations are often locked behind expensive, slow, and complex custom construction.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Cheap "big box" DIY kits are flimsy, not bioclimatic, and look low-end.
- Traditional wood pergolas require high maintenance and don't offer modern weather control.
- Local custom builds in Europe and North America can cost 3-4x more and take months to complete.
**Emotional tension:** Desire for a "quiet luxury" outdoor sanctuary without the headache of long-term construction or the look of a cheap kit.

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** Renson, Louveread — High-end European brands; extremely high price points.
**Secondary:** Traditional wooden pergolas, retractable fabric awnings — lack durability and smart bioclimatic features.
**Indirect:** Home renovation projects — competing for the same "home improvement" budget.

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- **25+ Years Experience**: Deep heritage in aluminum processing since 1995.
- **Premium DIY Kits**: Modular engineering designed for seamless self-assembly without sacrificing architectural quality.
- **Global Compliance**: Products engineered and tested to meet the rigorous snow and wind load standards of France, Germany, Canada, and the USA.
- **24-Hour Design Service**: Professional technical drawings and DIY layout plans provided within 24 hours.
- **Smart Ecosystem**: Modular integration of RGB lighting, wind/rain sensors, and heaters.
