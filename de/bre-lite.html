Interior Engineering

Master-Grade Culinary Layout:
Precision in Every Detail

The interior of the BRE-Lite is a masterclass in ergonomics and engineering. Every component, from the charcoal grill to the wine glass rack, is positioned to create a seamless professional cooking experience.

Integrated Back-Panel Lighting

Security & Protection

All-Weather Engineering:
Waterproof Integrity

Designed for year-round outdoor operation, the BRE-Lite features a redundant waterproofing system that protects both the intelligent electronics and the user's safety in high-humidity or rainy conditions.

Waterproof Control Center

All core electronics are housed in a dedicated, weather-sealed IP-rated electrical box, effectively isolating them from rain, dust, and coastal salt spray.

Leakage Protection (RCD)

Integrated high-sensitivity residual current device (RCD) provides an essential safety layer, instantly cutting power in the event of an electrical fault.

Sealed Wire Termination

All cable entries use compression-sealed glands to prevent moisture ingress, ensuring long-term connection integrity and preventing short circuits.

Structural Engineering

Unyielding Strength:
Architectural Grade Integrity

The BRE-Lite is not just a kitchen; it is a structural masterpiece. Built with a high-density carbon steel internal skeleton and aircraft-grade aluminum cladding, it is engineered to withstand the most demanding environmental loads.

850kg Static Mass

The sheer mass of the unit ensures absolute stability without the need for complex ground anchoring in most residential settings.

Extreme Schneelastkapazität

Reinforced roof structure and synchronized lift columns are designed to handle heavy snow accumulation without structural deformation.

Anti-Torsion Frame

The multi-point welded internal frame prevents twisting or misalignment, ensuring the electric door operates perfectly even after years of exposure.
