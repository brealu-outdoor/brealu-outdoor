Engineering The Perfect Blade

The BRE-175U represents the "Golden Mean" in louver engineering. By comparing our three core profiles, you can see the structural evolution from entry-level to heavy-duty performance.

Profile Tier Key Advantage
140U - Entry Lightweight Simplicity
175U - Elite Double-Skin Thermal Shield

Why Double-Skin?

The 175U's hollow-core design creates a structural air buffer, providing superior thermal insulation and sound dampening compared to single-layer blades.
