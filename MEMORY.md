## Design Preferences
- **Typography**: **Outfit** for headers, **Inter** for body text.
- **Color Palette**: Primary Blue (`#004A99`), Inquiry Orange (`#FF6600`), and secondary Green (`#00A651`).
- **Layout**: Hybrid approach. Header, Top Bar, and Banner (visuals + text) must strictly follow the original `index.html`. Logo height: 44px.
- **Breadcrumb Logic**: Standardized to `Home / Pergola / [Model]`. Applied across all pages (List, L2, and PDP).
- **Category Page**: 3-column grid (left) + 280px navigation sidebar (right). Alunotec-inspired aesthetic. **UI Update**: Individual product cards feature compact, centered "Inquire" buttons (#004A99). **Differentiated Entries**: BRE-220 (Wall-mount) and BRE-175U (Full Option) used to fill gaps, linking to their respective L2/PDP pages.
- **Gold Standard PDP Components**: 
    - **1:1 Main Gallery** (Main + 6 thumbs).
    - **Performance Grid**: 6-matrix (Waterproof, Wind, UV, Ventilation, 0-120°, Snow).
    - **Technical Comparison Table**: "Compare & Decide" style with model-specific data.
    - **Modular Customize Section**: Circular color swatches and add-on selectors (Fan, Heater, Weather Sensor).
- **Product Detail Page (PDP) Layout (11-Section Design)**: 
    1. **Gallery & Info**: 1:1 Main Gallery (Main + 6 thumbs) + Professional Info panel (long-form title, rating, attribute selectors, and orange "GET LATEST PRICE" button).
    2. **Overview**: High-impact hero section with architectural renders.
    3. **Main Features**: 5-core matrix (6063-T6 Aluminum, Sunshade, Hurricane, 100% Rainproof, Easy Installation).
    4. **Performance Data**: Detailed technical cards (AkzoNobel Qualicoat 2, Snow 80-115cm, Wind 180-220 km/h).
    5. **Customize**: 3-column grid (Shape/Size/Color).
    6. **Installation Methods**: Visual comparison (Freestanding vs. Wall-Mounted).
    7. **Side Components**: Accessories (Zip screen, Glass door, Aluminum shutter, others).
    8. **Drainage System**: Live Simulation (video) + Technical bullet points.
    9. **Why Choose Us**: Trust signals (Factory Direct, Certified, Expert Support, Logistics).
    10. **Portfolio/Reviews**: Bento Grid projects + Customer testimonials.
    11. **FAQ & Final CTA**: Technical Q&A + Dark-themed contact section.
