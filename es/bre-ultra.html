Frequently Asked
Questions

What does "Zero Montaje" really mean?

It means the BRE-Ultra arrives 100% factory-pre-built. There are no cabinets to assemble or grills to mount. You simply unpack it and connect your utilities.

Is the TV weather-protected?

Yes. The Smart TV is integrated behind the architectural lift-up door, which is sealed with industrial-grade weather gaskets to protect against rain and dust.

How is the dual-fuel grill operated?

The 6-burner system features dedicated zones for both gas and charcoal. You can use them independently or simultaneously for the ultimate flavor control.
