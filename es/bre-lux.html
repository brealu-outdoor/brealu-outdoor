Frequently Asked
Questions

How long is the Instalaci?

BRE-Lux is designed as a pre-integrated modular system. Professional Instalaci typically takes 4-6 hours for a standard L-shape setup.

Does it require a foundation?

Due to its 850kg architectural mass, it remains stable on any level, solid surface. Ground anchoring is recommended for high-wind areas.

Is the dual-fuel system safe?

Yes. The gas and charcoal sections are thermally isolated with secondary heat shields to ensure safe, simultaneous operation.
