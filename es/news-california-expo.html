Brealu Group at the California Design Expo

February 18, 2022 | Industry Events

The California Design Expo 2022 saw Brealu Group reaffirming its commitment to the North American market with a showcase of Advanced Aluminum Engineering. Our booth, featuring the new California Coastal palette, drew significant attention from US-based landscape architects and residential developers.

The highlight of our exhibition was the hands-on demonstration of our 6063-T5 Structural Integrityユ standard that defines the balance between architectural flexibility and high-tensile strength.

Innovation: 6063-T5 vs. T6

A common question at the Expo was our choice of temper. While T6 offers maximum hardness, 6063-T5 provides superior extrusion precision and Acabado quality, essential for the intricate "Lego-Logic" interlocking mechanisms of our louver systems. At the Expo, we demonstrated how our T5-tempered extrusions maintain absolute dimensional stability even under California's intense heat cycles.
