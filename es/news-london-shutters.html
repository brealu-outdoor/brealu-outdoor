Integrating Bioclimatic Shutters in London Terraces

September 05, 2023 | Urban Living

In the high-density luxury residential areas of Londonユrom Kensington to Canary Wharfユutdoor space is a rare premium. However, the proximity of neighbors often compromises the sense of sanctuary. The solution? Integrated Bioclimatic Shutters.

Our recent work in London highlights how the BRE-220 system, equipped with operable aluminum side shutters, transforms a exposed terrace into a private architectural room.

The Dual-Function of Bioclimatic Shutters

London's climate is notoriously unpredictable. Our integrated shutters provide a two-tier benefit: Visual Privacy and Draft Control. By adjusting the pitch of the side louvers, residents can block sightlines from neighboring buildings while maintaining natural cross-ventilationユ must for urban summer days.

Acoustic Dampening and Thermal Shielding

Beyond privacy, the reinforced aluminum louvers of the BRE-220 serve as a minor acoustic barrier, dampening the white noise of the London cityscape. In winter, when closed, the shutters act as a thermal shield, creating a micro-climate on the terrace that can be further enhanced with our integrated infrared heating systems.

This integration ensures that the "London Terrace" is no longer a seasonal luxury, but a permanent extension of the homeユ interior living area.
