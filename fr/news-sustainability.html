The Green Lifecycle: Recyclability of 6063 Aluminum

December 04, 2022 | Eco-Engineering

Brealu Group is committed to achieving net-zero impact across its manufacturing lifecycle. A core pillar of this commitment is our utilization of high-purity **6063-T5 and T6 aluminum alloys**, which are not only high-performing but also 100% recyclable without any loss of structural property.

A Circular Economy

The aluminum industry requires significantly less energyp to 95% lesso recycle secondary aluminum than to produce primary aluminum from ore. At our Foshan production facility, we maintain a closed-loop recycling program for all manufacturing offcuts, ensuring zero material waste.

When an architect chooses a Brealu pergola, they are choosing a structure that is designed to last 50+ years, and at the end of its long life, can be fully repurposed into new high-precision components. This is the definition of engineering for the future.
