Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between BRE-175U and BRE-220?

The BRE-175U uses a 175mm louver profile and is designed for standard residential to high-end estate projects with snow loads up to 80cm. The BRE-220 is our heavy-duty version with enhanced structural reinforcement for extreme spans and snow loads exceeding 100cm.

Can I customize the size?

Yes. The BRE-175U supports full customization to the nearest millimeter to ensure a perfect fit for your specific architectural footprint.

What is the warranty period?

The BRE-175U comes with a 15-year structural warranty and a 10-year surface finish warranty, backed by our global technical support team.
