Technical Q&A

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the benefit of the 220mm louver?

The 220mm "Aerofoil" louver is wider and stronger than standard residential models. It allows for longer spans between beams (up to 6m) and provides a more dramatic, architectural look while optimizing airflow and light control.

Is the system fully automated?

Yes. Every BRE-220 comes standard with a synchronized motorized drive system. You can control the roof via remote control, smartphone app, or integrate it with weather sensors for fully autonomous operation.

Can I install this myself?

While the BRE-220 is designed for professional results, it uses a modular assembly logic. For individuals with advanced DIY skills and proper equipment, it is possible, but we generally recommend a professional 2-person team for optimal results.

Can I customize the size for my specific project?

Absolutely. While we offer standard modules, the BRE-220 is specifically engineered for custom architectural integration. We can customize the dimensions to the nearest millimeter to ensure a perfect fit for your specific terrace or commercial space.

How is the product shipped and what is the lead time?

We ship globally using reinforced, sea-worthy wooden crate packaging to prevent any transit damage. The typical production lead time is 20-30 days. We provide comprehensive logistics support, including door-to-door delivery and customs handling for most regions.

What kind of after-sales support and warranty do you provide?

The BRE-220 is backed by our industry-leading 20-year structural warranty and a 15-year finish warranty. Furthermore, we provide lifetime technical support. Every unit's specifications are archived, allowing us to ship precise replacement parts or compatible accessories at any time in the future.
