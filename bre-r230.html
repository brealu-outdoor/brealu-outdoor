Expert Knowledge.

Is the R230 100% waterproof?
Yes. When closed, the interlocking louvers create a watertight seal. Rainwater is channeled through an internal gutter system and discharged via the corner posts.
How long does installation take?
A standard 4x7m R230 module typically requires 2-3 professional installers and can be completed in approximately 12-16 hours. We provide full technical blueprints.
Can it be integrated with Home Assistant?
Absolutely. Our smart motors support Zigbee and Tuya protocols, allowing for seamless integration with Google Home, Alexa, and professional KNX systems.
