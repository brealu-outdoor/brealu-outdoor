Questions & Answers

What is the service life of the motor?
Normally, the service life of the motor is more than 5 years. We recommend placing orders to purchase spare parts for after-sales needs to ensure continuous operation.
Is the system easy for DIY installation?
Yes, we provide specialized 3D installation videos and manuals. Each component is precision-cut at the factory for a perfect fit. Most residential projects can be completed in 2-3 days by two adults.
Can the size be customized beyond 7 meters?
Absolutely. While 4x7m is the single-module limit for 4 posts, our systems are modular. We can join multiple units together to cover any length required for commercial or large residential projects.
