BRE-R230 Retractable: The Future of Dynamic Shading

October 11, 2022 | Innovation Hub

The **BRE-R230 Flagship Retractable** system officially enters the global market, redefining the concept of "Sky Opening" panoramic views. Unlike traditional louvered systems that merely tilt, the R230 louvers stack completely at one end of the structure, providing a 100% unobstructed view of the sky.

Precision Motorized Engineering

Powered by our latest silent-drive synchronized motor system, the R230 can retract its 4.5-meter blades in under 30 seconds. The drive mechanism is fully concealed within the side beams, maintaining a clean architectural profile while delivering unmatched operational reliability.

Designed for premium hospitality and residential rooftop penthouses, the BRE-R230 is the ultimate statement of luxury and technical prowess in the Brealu portfolio.
